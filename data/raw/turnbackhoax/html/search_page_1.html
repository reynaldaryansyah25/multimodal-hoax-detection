Anti-Hoax Search Engine (ASE)

Our Anti-Hoax Search Engine (ASE) only searches trusted websites. Websites which contains fake news / hoaxes / hate-speech will not show up here.

For the sake of transparency, here is the list of the websites included in ASE : Trusted Sites List

Those websites fulfills the following criterias :
  • Anti-Hoax Database : at turnbackhoax.id
  • Registered Media websites : listed at Dewan Pers (press council) with status : "Terverifikasi Faktual dan Administrasi" (verified factually & administratively)
  • Trusted websites : such as Wikipedia, etc - with contents which can be easily traced & verified.
